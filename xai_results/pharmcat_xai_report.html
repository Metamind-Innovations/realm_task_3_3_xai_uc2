How to Read Importance Scores

Importance scores indicate how much each variant contributes to the phenotype prediction based on data-driven analysis of PharmCAT's behavior:

  • Base evidence (1.0): Every variant found in the VCF gets this score
  • Matching evidence (2.0): Variant is directly used in PharmCAT's haplotype matching
  • Uncallable haplotype evidence (2.0): Variant prevents calling specific haplotypes
  • Missing position evidence (1.0): Missing data affects available haplotype calls
  • Report evidence (2.0): Variant is referenced in the final phenotype report
  • Genotype evidence: Heterozygous (1.0) or Homozygous alternate (2.0)
  • Phenotype impact (1.0): Contributes to a definitive phenotype prediction

Higher scores (5.0+) indicate variants that significantly influence PharmCAT's phenotype determination.
